The Summer I Turned Pretty.

"The Summer I Turned Pretty" is a coming-of-age novel series by Jenny Han that revolves around the transformative summer experiences of the protagonist, Isabel "Belly" Conklin, as she spends summers at the beach with her mother's best friend and their two sons, Conrad and Jeremiah. Belly's childhood crush on Conrad develops into a complex love triangle leading to a summer of self-discovery, heartbreak, and personal growth. This emotionally charged series explores themes of first love, family dynamics, and the passage from adolescence to adulthood, offering readers a poignant and relatable journey through the ups and downs of youth.
